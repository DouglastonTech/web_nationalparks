Volcanoes National Park is on the Island of Hawai'i. It has been a national park since 1916.

The park is home to the world's largest active volcano, Mauna Loa!

Hawaii is located on top of a tectonic plate that keeps expanding under the Pacific Ocean. The flowing lava from Mauna Loa and Kilauea volcanoes has been adding to the island's landmass.
There is a whole new island being born!
